Welcome to Bunyonyi Paradise Resort

A Warm Welcome Awaits

Nestled along the breathtaking shores of Lake Bunyonyi, Bunyonyi Paradise Resort offers a peaceful escape surrounded by nature's beauty. Our resort is designed to provide comfort, relaxation, and unforgettable experiences for every guest. Whether you are seeking a quiet getaway, a romantic retreat, or an adventurous holiday, we offer exceptional hospitality, scenic views, and a serene environment. Enjoy the calm waters, fresh air, and warm Ugandan welcome as you create lasting memories with us.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Bunyonyi Paradise Resort is located on the beautiful shores of Lake Bunyonyi in southwestern Uganda. Surrounded by rolling hills and serene waters, the resort offers breathtaking views and a peaceful natural environment. We are approximately 6km from Kabale town, easily accessible by road.

Guests can enjoy a variety of activities including canoeing on Lake Bunyonyi, bird watching, nature walks, swimming, and relaxing by the lakeside. The resort also offers guided island-hopping tours, cultural village visits, hill hiking, sunset boat cruises, and day trips to nearby Bwindi Impenetrable Forest for gorilla trekking.

You can make a reservation by using the booking form on this page, contacting us via email at info@bunyonyiparadise.com, or calling our support team. Our staff is always ready to assist you with your booking and any inquiries. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak season (June–September).

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a one-night charge. For special packages and peak season bookings, different cancellation terms may apply — please contact us for full details.
