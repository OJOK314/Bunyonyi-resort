Activities
03 — Explore

Water Sports & Lake Activities

Lake Bunyonyi is one of East Africa's most beautiful lakes, and we give you every tool to explore it. From peaceful sunrise paddles to guided island-hopping tours, the lake is your playground.

  • Canoeing & kayaking hire
  • Guided island-hopping tours
  • Swimming & snorkelling areas
  • Sunset boat cruises

Tours
04 — Discover

Guided Tours & Cultural Visits

Go beyond the resort with our expert local guides. Visit the 29 islands of Lake Bunyonyi, learn about the Bakiga culture, discover endemic bird species, and walk trails that few tourists ever see.

  • Bird watching with field guides
  • Village & community cultural tours
  • Hill hiking & nature walks
  • Gorilla tracking transfers (Bwindi)
Plan Your Tour
